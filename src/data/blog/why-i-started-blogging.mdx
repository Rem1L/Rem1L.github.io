<En>
I've tried blogging before. It never stuck — too much time fiddling with site infrastructure, too much agonizing over every sentence. The overhead always outweighed the urge to write.

But lately I've been feeling it more: the value of having a place to put your thoughts out into the world. Even better if there's a small crowd of like-minded people to argue with.

What changed? Honestly, LLMs getting cheap. Token costs are low enough now that Claude Code can handle the annoying parts — formatting, prose cleanup, translation — without me having to think twice. It can even help pressure-test an argument while I'm building it.

So here we are. A blog. Mostly a record for myself, but the door's open.
</En>

<Zh>
过去我无法坚持blog写作，因为我既要应付网站的维护，又得花大把时间斟酌文字。维护成本始终压过了想写东西的冲动。

然而最近我越来越感觉到，有一个可以发表自己观点的空间是一件美妙的事情——当然最好有一群能够相互理解的家伙一起讨论。

改变的契机？说白了就是token越来越便宜了。现在Claude Code能轻易帮我解决排版、行文和翻译的问题，不用我多想。它还能帮我积极验证一些观点，充当一个随时在线的杠精。

于是blog就这么搭起来了。主要是浅作记录用，但欢迎来聊。
</Zh>
