<En>
I've tried blogging before. It never stuck — too much time fiddling with site infrastructure, too much agonizing over every sentence. The overhead always outweighed the urge to write.

But lately I've been feeling it more: when you have something to say and nowhere to put it, having a space of your own matters. Even better if there's a small crowd of like-minded people to argue with.

Conveniently, I had some spare credits lying around. Claude Code handles the annoying parts — formatting, prose cleanup, translation — and can even help stress-test an idea.

So the blog is up. Mostly just a record.
</En>

<Zh>
过去我无法坚持blog写作，因为我既要应付网站的维护，又得花大把时间斟酌文字。维护成本始终压过了想写东西的冲动。

然而最近我越来越感觉到，无法抒怀时有一个可以发表自己观点的空间是一件美妙的事情——当然最好有一群能够相互理解的家伙一起讨论。

正好手头有些多余的credit可以用。现在Claude Code能轻易帮我解决排版、行文和翻译的问题。还能帮我积极验证一些观点。

于是blog就这么搭起来了。且作记录用。
</Zh>
