<En>
[NDSS 2027 has a CFP.](https://www.ndss-symposium.org/ndss2027/submissions/call-for-papers/) It's happening. The CFP is out. Summer cycle deadline is **May 6, 2026** — about four weeks away. Fall cycle closes August 19.

The conference itself is listed only as "2027," with no specific dates or location yet. Which is a little funny — we have submission deadlines before we have a venue. But at this point I'll take confirmed-in-some-form over the alternative.

If you're submitting: summer cycle camera-ready is September 30. Fall cycle camera-ready is January 6, 2027. Plan accordingly.
</En>
