<En>
[NDSS 2027 has a CFP.](https://www.ndss-symposium.org/ndss2027/submissions/call-for-papers/) That's more than we could say with confidence a few months ago.

ISOC — the Internet Society, which runs NDSS — went through some turbulent restructuring over the past year or two. Staff layoffs, budget uncertainty, the usual nonprofit drama. NDSS is one of their flagship events, and for a while the community was quietly asking whether NDSS 27 would actually materialize. Nobody said it out loud much, but the question was there.

So: it's happening. The CFP is out. Summer cycle deadline is **May 6, 2026** — about four weeks away. Fall cycle closes August 19.

The conference itself is listed only as "2027," with no specific dates or location yet. Which is a little funny — we have submission deadlines before we have a venue. But at this point I'll take confirmed-in-some-form over the alternative.

If you're submitting: summer cycle camera-ready is September 30. Fall cycle camera-ready is January 6, 2027. Plan accordingly.
</En>

<Zh>
[NDSS 2027 的 CFP 出来了。](https://www.ndss-symposium.org/ndss2027/submissions/call-for-papers/) 这件事搁几个月前还真不是理所当然的。

组织 NDSS 的是 ISOC（Internet Society），过去一两年里他们经历了一轮比较明显的收缩——裁员、预算压力、非盈利组织常见的那种内部动荡。NDSS 是他们最重要的旗舰会议之一，社区里私下都在讨论 NDSS 27 究竟还会不会办。没人大声说，但问题摆在那里。

所以现在好了，CFP 出来了，会议确定要办。Summer cycle 截稿是 **2026 年 5 月 6 日**，差不多还有四周。Fall cycle 截稿是 8 月 19 日。

会议本身的具体日期和地点暂时还没有，只写了"2027 年举办"。有点好笑——截稿日期有了，会开在哪还不知道。不过比起"可能根本不办"，这已经是好结果了。

打算投稿的：Summer cycle camera-ready 是 9 月 30 日，Fall cycle camera-ready 是 2027 年 1 月 6 日。
</Zh>
