Rem1L is an adversarial LLM engineer with a focus on LLM security research. 

**Research Interests**

- **Indirect Prompt Injection (IPI)** — attack and defense for LLM-based agents
- **Access Control in Agentic Systems** — permission boundaries and isolation in multi-agent architectures
- **Traditional Defenses vs. LLM-assisted Attacks** — evaluating the effectiveness of classical security mechanisms under LLM-powered threat models
- **LLM-assisted Vulnerability Discovery** — leveraging LLM capabilities to uncover novel vulnerability classes

Currently pursuing a PhD at Ohio State University (OSU). Open to interesting research collaborations and projects.

Feel free to reach out via GitHub.
